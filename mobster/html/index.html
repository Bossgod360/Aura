Why Earth 2.0?

Everything has its own limitations and our Earth is quickly approaching hers. We can reverse these changes by terraforming. With the aid of our terra-bots and other advanced tools in geoengineering, we will not only be able to help the Earth recover faster but also advance into making other planets like Venus or Mars more habitable.

Our growing population will soon get out of hand and we'll need more space. Geoengineering an existing planet will create a new place to live for more of humanity. We will also gain more resources through ecologically extracting the minerals from the planet. Our company isn’t just a solution to the biggest problems that we face today but also provides support for humans to take the next big leap into the future.

What is Terrforming

Terraforming is a process in which we reform a planet’s ecology or topography to make it more suitable for life in the simplest of terms. To go more in-depth this process will unfold in 5 key steps.
... Firstly, we need a base for our earth-bound human respondents where they'd operate the terra-bots. The second step would be to make the planet’s atmosphere and magnetic field more suitable for creatures to survive. Thirdly, we would need to regulate the temperature over some time until it’s stable. Next, we would start the process of which we change the terrain of the planet to make oceans and land for infrastructure. Lastly, we'd need to introduce human life to the planet. We'd need to make sure that everything is correctly balanced and create a civilization that is self-sustainable.
